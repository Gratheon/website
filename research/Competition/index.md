## Competitive Landscape Overview

The precision beekeeping market is validated and growing, with competitors across multiple segments:
- **Robotic apiaries** - Full automation (enterprise market)
- **Entrance observers** - Computer vision monitoring (Gratheon's direct competition)
- **IoT sensors** - Basic monitoring (established market)
- **Web apps** - Digital hive management (software-only)

### Gratheon's Competitive Advantages
- **Frame-level internal inspection** (unique, no competitor offers this)
- **Integrated ecosystem** (entrance + frame + sensors + app)
- **Middle market pricing** (â‚¬200-400 vs â‚¬350-550)
- **European focus** (local advantage)

### Key Strategic Insights
- Speed to market is critical (launch before ApicAI expands features)
- Frame inspection is strongest differentiator
- European semi-pros are underserved market segment
- Hardware + software integration creates competitive moat
- Community building and partnerships essential

Fullstack engineer

Thanks for applying to the Gratheon Full Stack engineer volunteer position. I assume you want to help bees and beekeepers have an easier life while working on  tech like AI, IoT, robots and contributing to opensource community. Ultimately we want to build an autonomous robot that can take care of bees. 

Just wanted to reiterate that this is not a paid position. We're slowly developing the project in our spare time and our team is composed of volunteers, small (5-10) and informal. I wrote about volunteering and people's motivation here -> https://gratheon.com/about/Volunteers%20%E2%9D%A4%EF%B8%8F%E2%80%8D%F0%9F%94%A5/

The current status of the project is that it has a legal body, no income, around 100 registered users. We have a web-app and PWA mobile app written in react that is open for anyone to register and try out at https://app.gratheon.com/. We‚Äôve had various hackathons and accelerators so far. The last one was prototron.ee where we got in the top 25 out of 50 teams.

We try to build a special culture ( https://gratheon.com/about/company/Culture%20and%20values%20%F0%9F%AB%80/ ) and because of that our code, datasets, CAD models, internal docs are fully open source, and you‚Äôre encouraged to check them out at https://github.com/Gratheon

So if you have time for a side project I suggest you to:

1. Explore what you think is best you can do technically and what interests you the most out of our list of ideas for new features we have on our website https://gratheon.com/about/products/%F0%9F%93%B1Web-app/

2. Join Discord to discuss a specific feature you could work on, but which is not clear / not groomed yet: https://discord.gg/PcbP4uedWj

3. Once you decided what you want to work on, do some coding, creating a PR for code. You can follow engineering onboarding to have app running locally. https://gratheon.com/docs/web-app/

Some of the features need only frontend work, most need storing data on the backend. Some features need work with AI model training, video streaming, time-series DBs etc. 

Feel free to explore the website and I hope to hear back from you in the Discord.

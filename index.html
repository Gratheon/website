State of beekeeping

Honeybees are cute and fluffy, making up a super-organism that we want to take care of, globally. They are key to natural pollination and our food supply. However, hobby beekeeping is challenging as it requires constant monitoring to keep track of varroa mites, presense of queen, hornet attacks, swarming or stealing state.

Traditional beekeepers do hard physical labor and inspect beehives on a weekly basis. Inspections can lead to colony stress and loss of bees due to stinging or alcohol washing tests.

Existing modern solutions on the market are inadequate because they focus on low-cost temperature and sound sensors that provide insufficient information. Alternative solutions target industrial beekeeping and focus on unhealthy congestion of multiple bee colonies inside a shipping container. Industrial beekeeping often exploits bees by moving colonies around mono-culture fields, draining the lifespan of worker bees.

Open Beekeeping assistance system

Our long-term vision is a single-colony autonomous robotic beehive, powered by deep reinforcement machine learning, with multiple cameras and sensors, providing advanced monitoring to the beekeeper along with remote control, removing the need of manual inspections and making beekeeping more ethical.

Features

We want to achieve:

  • Monitoring. Give better insights on colony life by analyzing nursing and field bees demographics
    • ✅ Upload images of beehive frames
    • ✅ Detection of bees on a hive frame based on a photo
    • ✅ Analyze brood development and hive resources
    • ⏳ Detection of queen presense
    • ⏳ Detection of swarming based on queen cups
  • Longevity of bee colony and individual worker bees.
    • ⏳ Detect hornets
    • ⏳ Detect varroa destructor mites
  • Autonomous hive
    • ⏳ Use robotic box and frame actuators
    • ⏳ Entrance control, depending on hornets & weather
  • Modern digital app
    • ✅ Visually manage beehive and its frames using a web app
    • ✅ Plantnet integration with list of local plants
    • ✅ Weather forecast around apiary
And much more - see our roadmap and tasks. Our system is open source. You can improve our system or build upon it yourself. We provide GraphQL API. Feel free to import or export data.

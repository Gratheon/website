Business risk protection plan

Intellectual property

To protect 🍯Products from copy-cats and infringement of company 🧪Brand, we are using IP protection mechanisms:

  • Logo & name is going to be trademarked
  • Hardware devices (method of operation) are going to be patented
  • Hardware design and UI design (unique external look) is going to be patented

Product moat

To reduce churn and have “Product moat” stickiness of the product we focus on:

  • Continous collection of 💽Datasets from user data and keeping it private for improving model precision.
    • Only part of datasets (collected directly by the company in the field) are going to be public.
  • Holistic approach to beekeeping gives us a competitive edge
    • full vertical product ownership with hardware makes “moat” wider
    • multiple AI models used allows us to be flexible in detecting various objects
    • horizontal feature variability 💡Ideas and Features
  • Intangible asset. 🧪Brand should give a feeling of high-quality care for the bees 🐝
  • Network effect. By building a community of beekeepers we increase stickyness - 🧑🏻‍🚀Beekeepers for field testing, 🎒Academy, ♻️Share of beehive inspection public URL, ♻️Public beehive view
  • Switching cost. We do not limit user data extraction, but you do need to write a software to explicitly export data via API.
    • We also want to integrate with existing analog solutions to gather their data and act as a data platform for beekeeping

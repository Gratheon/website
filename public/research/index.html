🔬 Research

We want to be aware of recent and historical development in research that is tied to bees, observability and machine learning. We are open to collaboration with research and academic institutions (see our docs for more engineering and ML specific articles).

Here we track research papers that are most relevant. For practical and commercial projects see Competition

Research collaboration with us​

  • Tartu University - We are open to consulting on bachelor and master degrees work
  • TalTech / Vidrik - we have collaborated on a whitepaper

Teams​

World-wide there are several research teams dealing in the area of digital beekeeping

  • 🇨🇿 Brno University. Simon Bilik, Adam Ligocki
  • 🇩🇪 Karlsruhe Institute of Technology. Lead - Tausch F. Project name - Apic.ai https://github.com/apic-ai
  • 🇺🇸 Puerto Rico team with Rémi Megret as the lead. Project name - BigDBee / LabelBee https://github.com/rmegret/labelbee
  • 🇺🇸 Utah State University team with Kulyukin Vladimir as the lead. Project name - BeePi. https://github.com/VKEDCO/PYPL/tree/master/beepi/py
  • 🇦🇹 Graz University - Thomas Schmickl
  • 🇧🇷 Federal University of Itajuba in Brazil - Marcelo Rovai, José Alberto Ferreira Filho

One-time yet very notable works were also in:

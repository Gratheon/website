Unit economics of beekeepers

How beekeepers loose money

  • Equipment
    • Hives
      • Full single hive (without bees) ~ 150 EUR
      • Deep sections.
        Lifetime ~ 5-20 years, depends if its painted
      • Paint for protection. ~ 30 EUR for ~ 10 sections
      • Frames with new wax comb ~ 2 EUR / frame.
        Lifetime ~ 3 years
    • Honey badger ~ 300 - 800 EUR. Depends on size and if its motorized
    • Wax melter ~2500 EUR (source 2023)
    • Suit, Gloves
  • Bees (aka bee package on 4 frames) ~ 150 EUR
  • Buying queens
  • Transport (to distant apiaries, mobile apiaries)
  • Labour costs (time & sweat)
    • Inspections
    • Managing hive equipment
    • Waste management
    • Respirator for treatment
    • Waterproof gloves for treatment
  • Treatment
    • ApiGuard. ~75 EUR, 20 doses (source 2021)
    • Epipen ~ 50 EUR (source 2021)

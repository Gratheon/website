👁️‍🗨️ Entrance Observer

status: alpha, TRL 5

💡 Entrance Observer is a set of cameras and a hardware devices that analyze beehive entrance. It records a video stream, runs AI analysis that sends metrics and videos to our web app for preview and alerts.

Check and install our open source code of entrance-observer

Problem focus​

Out of all beekeeping issues, this product is helping with these:

  • 💢 Hornet attacks
  • 💢 Robbing state
  • 🦀 Infestations
  • 🧶 Swarming
  • 🦀 Diseases
  • 💀 Colony without a queen
  • 🤢 Pesticide poisoning

Features​

  • 🎥 Video streaming via API
  • 🐝 Detect swarming
  • 🎮 Client-side app with UI
  • 📈 Count bees coming in and out - on the edge
  • 📊 Bee movement metric reporting
  • 🛣️ Landing board heatmap generation

Version history​

Prototype v4 at field testing. Camera has protective case. Missing the landing board protective cone as it needs artificial lighting

Prototype v3. Added protective cone, now idea is to have CPU/GPU block separated and moved into the hive roof to have less devices on the entrance, have camera integrated with the cone, have WIFI antennae and power blocks farther away from the bees.

Prototype v1 version. Camera at the center on a monopod, jetson orin nano at the center with wiring and antennae facing down

Robotic beehive

Why

To solve Observability of bee colony, Hard physical labor and Remote apiary locations take time problems, we‚Äôre building a robotic beehive, capable of replacing human beekeeper by doing inspections automatically.

How

Robot should extract hive frames, take a photo and upload to the cloud app where it will be processed for health status. Extraction should happen without disturbing the bees, exposing them to elements or risking people around.

This is a fixed single-colony robot and is a base functionality thatü§ñRobotic apiary beekeeper would use to serve entire apiary. Pricing strategy is to follow Tesla approach - first build an expensive prototype from generic parts and over time make it more affordable
